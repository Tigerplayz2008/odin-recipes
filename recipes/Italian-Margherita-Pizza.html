Classic Italian Margherita Pizza

Description:

Indulge in the timeless flavors of a Classic Italian Margherita Pizza, a delicious and iconic dish that represents the simplicity and freshness of Italian cuisine. This recipe features a thin and crispy pizza crust topped with a vibrant combination of ripe tomatoes, fragrant basil leaves, and creamy mozzarella cheese. The Margherita Pizza is a true celebration of the finest ingredients, delivering an authentic taste of Italy.

Preparation time: 2 hours (including dough rising time)

Cooking time: 15-20 minutes

Yield: 2-4 servings

Ingredints, Pizza Dough:

  • 2 1/4 cups (280 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water (around 110째F/43째C)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Ingredints, Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  • 8 ounces (225 grams) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced or torn into pieces
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil, for drizzling

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and active dry yeast. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Stir the ingredients together until a shaggy dough forms. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Shape the dough into a ball and place it in a greased bowl. Cover the bowl with a clean kitchen towel and let the dough rise in a warm, draft-free place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Transfer it to a floured surface and divide it into two equal portions. Cover the portions with a clean kitchen towel and let them rest for about 10 minutes.
  7. Roll out each portion of the dough into a thin circle or rectangle, depending on your preference. If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the rolled-out dough onto a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Spread half of the pizza sauce or marinara sauce evenly over each pizza crust, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Arrange the fresh mozzarella cheese slices or torn pieces evenly on top of the sauce. Add the sliced tomatoes, distributing them evenly.
  10. Season the pizzas with salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle torn basil leaves over the top.
  11. If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizzas onto the preheated stone in the oven. If using a baking sheet, place the sheet in the oven.
  12. Bake the pizzas for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust turns golden brown and crispy, and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Remove the pizzas from the oven and let them cool for a minute. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
  14. Slice the Classic Italian Margherita Pizzas into wedges or squares and serve hot. Enjoy the vibrant flavors of this traditional Italian delight.
