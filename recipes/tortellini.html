Josephs World Famous Tortellini Recipe!

Description:

Indulge in the delightful flavors of Cheesy Tortellini, a mouthwatering pasta dish that combines tender tortellini with a rich and creamy cheese sauce. This recipe features plump and flavorful cheese-filled pasta, smothered in a velvety sauce made with a blend of cheeses and aromatic herbs. The Cheesy Tortellini Delight is a comforting and satisfying meal that will have you coming back for seconds

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredints:

  • 1 pound (450 grams) cheese tortellini
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the cheese tortellini according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Sprinkle the flour over the melted butter and garlic. Stir constantly for 1-2 minutes to cook the flour and form a roux.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Continue to cook, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens and comes to a simmer.
  5. Reduce the heat to low. Stir in the shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese, allowing them to melt into the sauce.
  6. Add the dried basil and dried oregano to the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  7. Add the cooked cheese tortellini to the saucepan, gently tossing to coat the pasta evenly with the cheese sauce.
  8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the tortellini to absorb some of the sauce and become heated through.
  9. Remove from heat and let the flavors meld for a minute or two.
  10. Serve the Cheesy Tortellini Delight in bowls, garnished with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  11. Enjoy this irresistible dish as a comforting main course. You can serve it with a side of garlic bread and a green salad to complete the meal.
